DEVELOPMENT OF BUDDYBONDS
From picking up Java:The Complete Reference by Herbert Schildt for the first time in college library to today, I feel like I have learned a quite about life in general. It was this book that got me interested in programming in the first place. Then searching the web for web development tutorials brought me to the Bucky's tutorials on YouTube.I got introduced to amazing basic stuff on w3schools website. On a random day in February 2018, I came up with an idea to build a simple and secure social network.But it had to be not-so confusing,so i kept it simple with php.
And then it took me some hell of a work to build this platform on which you are reading this page today.There were some obvious difficulties on the way but the work was really exciting and that is why I proudly present to you the modern network-buddyBonds.
